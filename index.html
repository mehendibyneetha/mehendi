With over 15 years of experience, I have dedicated my entire professional journey to the art of mehendi. Specializing in intricate bridal designs, I strive to create work that beautifully blends tradition, artistry, and personal storytelling.

Having served 3000+ happy clients and trained 100+ aspiring artists, I regularly invest in advanced training with leading mehendi artists across India, including Harin Dalal, to stay at the forefront of evolving styles and techniques. This enables me to offer distinctive bridal designs that are not commonly available in the Bangalore market.

Every bridal creation is carefully personalized to reflect the bride’s individuality, ensuring her mehendi is as unique as the story it represents.
